Best Educational Hack

Quake Shake is an educational game that teaches kids earthquake safety in a fun and interactive way. It covers all major aspects of dealing with earthquakes, from preparation, what to do in multifarious situations during an earthquake, and dealing with the aftermath. I created Quake Shake in Godot after researching earthquakes and discovering that typical earthquake safety taught by schools is inadequate.
