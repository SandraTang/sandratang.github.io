HeartAcuity

your personal heart health helper


What does HeartAcuity do?

HeartAcuity measures interprets your heartbeat data and sends out an alert if you are consistently predicted to be at risk of any heart diseases, including some that are normally unexpected such as ischemic strokes. The app also provides info on how heart disease and how to prevent it, including tips on healthy eating and physical fitness. You can set your own goals through the app and track your journey to and achieve your ideal level of health.

How does it work?

HeartAcuity interprets your heartbeats and basic info with numerous machine learning models to predict your probability of having specific heart diseases. HeartAcuity was created during HackMIT 2020 by Abishek Ashok, Sandra Tang, Sabrina Romero, and Eliot Huh over the course of 36 hours.

Is my data safe?

Yes, your data is safe! HeartAcuity will not send it anywhere. Heartbeat data is only sent from your Apple Watch to our app to help ur inform you about your heart health. The data is never sent to us or anyone else, and it is never sold.
