DISCLAIMER

If you wish to address the City Council, please complete this online public comment registration process prior to the time the Mayor calls for Public Comment. When you are recognised by the Mayor, please come to the podium and state your name for the record. There is a five (5) minute time limit per person, unless waived by the City Council. Under the Brown Act, the City Council is prohibited from discussing or taking action on any item not listed on the posted agenda.
