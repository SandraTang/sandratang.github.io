Featured Project

Quake Shake, Same Home Different Hacks Best Educational Hack 2020


Over the second weekend of June 2020, I solo-developed Quake Shake, an educational game that teaches kids earthquake safety in a fun and interactive way. It covers all major aspects of dealing with earthquakes, from preparation, what to do in multifarious situations during an earthquake, and dealing with the aftermath.
