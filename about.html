Hi! I'm Sandra Tang, a rising sophmore at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) majoring in Computer Science (Course 6-3), minoring in Design (under the Architecture Department, Course 4), and concentrating in Chinese. On campus, I serve as a chair for H Entry, my section of the dorm which has been affectionately nicknamed "HEX". In my free time, I enjoy calling friends, working out while watching Netflix, and try baking new recipes. My hobbies include creating video games and creating art.
